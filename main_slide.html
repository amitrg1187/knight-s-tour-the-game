How to Play Knight's Tour

Master the moves and complete the ultimate chess puzzle!

The Goal The objective of the Knight's Tour is to move a knight on a chessboard such that it visits every square exactly once. The game ends when all squares are visited (a successful tour) or when the knight has no more legal moves (a failed tour).

Knight's Movement

The knight moves in an "L" shape:
Two squares in one cardinal direction (horizontally or vertically). Then one square in a perpendicular direction. This means from any square, a knight can have up to 8 possible moves.
